Building Youth Leadership for Community Progress

MORELYA is a youth-led civic organization dedicated to empowering young leaders, strengthening community participation, and supporting inclusive local governance in Libertad.

About MORELYA

MORESCO-1 Libertad Youth in Action (MORELYA) is a dynamic platform dedicated to empowering the youth and nurturing future leaders. The organization encourages young individuals to actively participate in volunteerism, civic engagement, and community service. MORELYA promotes civic responsibility by fostering collaboration with local institutions, schools, government agencies, and non-profit organizations. Through these partnerships, members gain opportunities to contribute meaningfully to social initiatives and sustainable community projects.

The organization offers a variety of programs, including capacity-building workshops, leadership training, and advocacy activities. These programs help develop essential skills such as teamwork, ethical decision-making, and problem-solving, preparing youth to take on leadership roles in their communities. By participating in MORELYA, young individuals are empowered to innovate, lead, and create positive change. The organization aims to build a generation of socially-conscious citizens who actively contribute to local development, community welfare, and nation-building.
